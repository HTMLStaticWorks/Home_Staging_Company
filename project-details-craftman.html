Suburban Craftsman

Pasadena, CA | Sold for $150k Over Asking

Project Overview

This charming craftsman home had beautiful original wood detailing but felt dated and dark. To bridge the gap between historic charm and modern living, we chose a transitional design approach that blended classic silhouettes with light, contemporary fabrics.

By brightening the color palette and using mirrors to bounce light through the traditional dining area, we transformed a "heavy" room into an airy, welcoming space that appealed to growing families looking for a turnkey home.
