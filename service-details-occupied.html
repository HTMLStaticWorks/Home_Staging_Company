Occupied Home Styling

Enhancing your lived-in home with professional designer touches to attract high-value offers.

Living in Your Home While Selling?

Our Occupied Home Styling service is designed specifically for homeowners who remain in their properties during the listing period. We strategically blend your existing furniture with our designer accessories, artwork, and textiles to create a cohesive, aspirational look that appeals to the widest range of buyers.

The Lumina "Edit" Phase

01

The Decluttering Edit

We walk through your home and identify which personal items and small furnishings should be packed away to maximize visual space and focus buyers on the house itself.

02

Space Reconfiguration

Our designers rearrange your existing furniture to optimize traffic flow, emphasize architectural focal points (like fireplaces or views), and make rooms feel larger in listing photos.

03

Interior Layering

We bring in our "Styling Kit"—a curated collection of pillows, throws, high-end bedding, and tabletop accessories that transform a lived-in room into a designer showcase.
