Our Story

Designing for ROI.

The Art of Staging

At Lumina, we view staging not just as decorating, but as strategic market positioning. A vacant property is a blank canvas, but for most buyers, an empty space leaves them uninspired. We apply psychology, design principles, and deep real estate market knowledge to craft narratives within each home we stage.

🎨

Curated Inventory

We don't use dated "rental" furniture. We constantly update our expansive warehouse with pieces you'd find in luxury boutique hotels and high-end retail.

📈

Market Driven

Every design relies on buyer demographics. We study comparable sales to target the precise emotional appeal specific neighborhoods demand.

⚡

Flawless Execution

Real estate moves fast. Our logistics team ensures on-time delivery, quick installation, and perfectly clean removal for entirely stress-free transactions.

Meet the Principal

Sarah Jennings, Founder & Lead Designer

With over a decade of experience as a high-end interior designer and an active real estate investor, Sarah founded Lumina Staging to bridge the gap between livable aesthetic and salable product.

"A home is the largest asset most people will ever sell. Leaving the presentation of that asset to chance, or showing it completely empty, is leaving money on the table. Our mission is to protect that investment through beautiful, calculated design."
