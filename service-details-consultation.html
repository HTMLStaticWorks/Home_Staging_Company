Pre-Listing Consultation

A professional walkthrough and customized roadmap for sellers who want to maximize ROI through strategic self-execution.

Get the Plan, Do the Work, See the Returns.

Our Pre-Listing Consultation is a intensive, 2-hour professional assessment designed for homeowners who prefer a DIY approach but want the strategic guidance of an expert stager. We identify the specific changes that will provide the highest return on investment for your particular property.

What's Covered

01

The Walkthrough Assessment

We walk through your home room-by-room, examining lighting, flooring, wall colors, and general repairs through the lens of a prospective buyer.

02

Color & Material Audit

We provide specific brand and color recommendations for walls, cabinets, and flooring that will brighten the space and modernize the property's overall feel.

03

The Actionable Roadmap

Following the consultation, you receive a detailed, room-by-room checklist outlining every recommended change, prioritize by impact and budget.
