Vacant Property Staging

Bringing life and scale to empty spaces to maximize buyer emotional connection.

Why Stage an Empty House?

Empty rooms often appear smaller than they really are, and buyers struggle to emotionally connect with bare walls and empty floors. Our vacant staging service provides a lifestyle vision that allows potential buyers to imagine themselves living in the space.

The Process

01

Initial Consultation & Measurement

We begin with a thorough walkthrough of the property, taking detailed measurements and photos while assessing the architectural style, target demographic, and neighborhood comparable sales.

02

Custom Design Plan

Our team curates a specific inventory list selecting furniture, art, rugs, and accessories from our extensive warehouse that perfectly complement the home's aesthetic.

03

Installation & Styling

During the installation day, our logistics team and lead designers install and meticulously style every room down to the last decorative book and throw pillow, readying the home for professional photography.

What's Included

  • ✓ Kitchen styling (counters/island)
  • ✓ Primary Bedroom suite
